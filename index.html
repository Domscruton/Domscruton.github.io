Bio

I currently work at Fintern, a Fintech start-up leveraging Open Banking (OB) data to improve access to credit and develop a SAAS OB Credit Risk platform. I previously worked as a Graduate Data Scientist in Household Risk & Retail Pricing at Admiral Group PLC, a FTSE 100 Insurance firm. My work involved optimizing the pricing structure through advanced and traditional Statistical Modelling and Machine Learning approaches.

Challenging Coding and Statistical problems particularly interest me. Aside from work I referee Rugby Union, with experience at Durham University and the Scottish Midlands and Cardiff Societies.
